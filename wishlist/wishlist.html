Our Mission

At Head Injury Rehabilitation and Referral Services, Inc. (HIRRS), we provide people with acquired brain injuries and neurological disorders with comprehensive, high quality, individualized services which assist them in achieving their highest potential in their communities.

All donations to HIRRS are used by the agency, by our clients, in our programs or homes.

HIRRS does not sell items to third-parties and we are therefore only able to accept donations that are new or in excellent condition.

As a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit agency, donations to HIRRS are tax deductible to the extent of the law.

Vehicles

HIRRS accept vehicles in good operating condition to transport our clients.
