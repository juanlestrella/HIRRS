Donation

Our Mission

At Head Injury Rehabilitation and Referral Services, Inc. (HIRRS), we provide people with acquired brain injuries and neurological disorders with comprehensive, high quality, individualized services which assist them in achieving their highest potential in their communities.

As a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit agency, donations to HIRRS are tax deductible to the extent of the law. HIRRS accepts cash, checks, stocks or other assets. Other ways to support us is by donating through our Paypal, Venmo, CashApp, Google Pay, and Apple Pay accounts. Please use the buttons below to donate!
